Get to know me.

My name is Emily and I'm an aspiring web designer and developer. I earned my Bachelor's Degree in 2017 in Animation and Illustration from DePaul University. After some years working in the video production world, I decided I needed a career change.

In June of this year (2020), I completed Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp to add to my technical skillset and jumpstart a career in web development. I have a passion for art, design, technology, an eye for detail and a desire to create user-centered websites. As a LinkedIn Learning professor once told me, "Fall in love with the user".
